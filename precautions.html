Precautions to Follow

  • Wash your hands frequently with soap and water for at least 20 seconds.
  • Wear a mask in crowded or enclosed spaces.
  • Maintain a distance of at least 6 feet from others in public spaces.
  • Avoid touching your face, especially your eyes, nose, and mouth.
  • Cover your mouth and nose with a tissue or elbow when coughing or sneezing.
  • Disinfect frequently touched surfaces daily.
  • If you feel unwell, stay home and consult a healthcare provider.
